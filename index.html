About Me

Born in the shadow of an erupting volcano, I trained in the ways of nature learning to bend the elements to my will and change my form into animals. I have faced the nefarious Dragon Cult and prevented the rise of Tiamat, Mother of Dragons. I have entered the twisting, mysterious passageways of White Plume Mountain and returned to tell my tale...
in Dungeons & Dragons.

In my more mundane real life, I was born on a military base in Darmstadt, Germany (in the shadow of Frankenstein's castle! not really.) I am a 28 year old artist and writer. I graduated summa cum laude from the University of Colorado, Boulder with a Bachelor's in English. I have worked in youth programs, marijuana dispensaries and a literary arts magazine. I am a proud Wiccan.

With the help of the Goddess I am seeking a new path in life. Interested in learning how to code and expanding my career opportunities, I enrolled in a Coding Bootcamp at the University of Denver. This site is an exercise I am undertaking to advance my skills in HTML and CSS.
